when importing scenes and animations, these suffix to the names will make your
life easier:
	animatin:
		Animation loop (-loop, -cycle)
		example: my_animation-loop
		
	Mesh:
		Create collisions (-col, -convcol, -colonly, -convcolonly)
		example: my_terrain-col
		
		Create navigation (-navmesh)
		Create a VehicleBody (-vehicle)
		Create a VehicleWheel (-wheel)
		Rigid Body (-rigid)

Source: https://docs.godotengine.org/en/stable/tutorials/assets_pipeline/importing_3d_scenes/node_type_customization.html
